

## ArnoldCatMap
Arnold’s cat map: linear automorphism of the torus with hyperbolic dynamics and
uniform stretching and folding.

### State Equations
$$
x = \left(y + 2*x\right) \bmod 1
$$
$$
y = \left(x + y\right) \bmod 1
$$
