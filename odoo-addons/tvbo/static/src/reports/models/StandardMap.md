

## StandardMap
Chirikov standard map (area-preserving map on the 2-torus). At k≈0.971635 the golden
invariant torus breaks. Equations are expressed with modulo 2π on both variables.

### State Equations
$$
\theta = \left(p + \theta + k*\sin{\left(\theta \right)}\right) \bmod 2*\pi
$$
$$
p = \left(p + k*\sin{\left(\theta \right)}\right) \bmod 2*\pi
$$

### Parameters

| **Parameter** | **Value** | **Unit** | **Description** |
|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| $k$ | 0.971635 | N/A | Nonlinearity parameter controlling torus breakup. |
