

## TentMap
Piecewise-linear tent map on [0,1]. At μ=2 it is conjugate to the logistic map with r=4.

### State Equations
$$
x = \begin{cases} \mu*x & \text{for}\: x < 0.5 \\\mu*\left(1 - x\right) & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}
$$

### Parameters

| **Parameter** | **Value** | **Unit** | **Description** |
|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| $\mu$ | 2.0 | N/A | Slope parameter μ ∈ [0,2]. |
