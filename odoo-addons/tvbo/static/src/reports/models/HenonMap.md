

## HenonMap
The Hénon map is a two-dimensional discrete-time dynamical system exhibiting a strange
attractor for the default parameters a=1.4, b=0.3. It serves as a simple model for
chaotic dynamics in smooth invertible maps.

### State Equations
$$
x = 1 + y - a*x^{2}
$$
$$
y = b*x
$$

### Parameters

| **Parameter** | **Value** | **Unit** | **Description** |
|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| $a$ | 1.4 | N/A | Quadratic nonlinearity coefficient a. |
| $b$ | 0.3 | N/A | Linear contraction coefficient b. |
