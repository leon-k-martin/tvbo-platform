

## StuartLandauOscillator
Normal form of supercritical Hopf bifurcation in Cartesian coordinates; radius
dynamics decouple giving stable limit cycle of radius sqrt(mu) for mu > 0.

### State Equations
$$
\dot{x} = x*\left(\mu - x^{2} - y^{2}\right) - \omega*y - b*y*\left(x^{2} + y^{2}\right)
$$
$$
\dot{y} = \omega*x + y*\left(\mu - x^{2} - y^{2}\right) + b*x*\left(x^{2} + y^{2}\right)
$$

### Parameters

| **Parameter** | **Value** | **Unit** | **Description** |
|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| $\mu$ | 1.0 | N/A | Linear growth (bifurcation) parameter μ. |
| $\omega$ | 1.0 | N/A | Base angular frequency ω. |
| $b$ | 1.0 | N/A | Nonlinear frequency shift coefficient b. |
