

## MannevilleSimpleMap
Simple intermittency map x_{n+1} = [(1+ε)x_n + (1-ε)x_n^2] mod 1, used to illustrate
bursts separated by quiescent phases.

### State Equations
$$
x = \left(x*\left(1 + \epsilon\right) + x^{2}*\left(1 - \epsilon\right)\right) \bmod 1
$$

### Parameters

| **Parameter** | **Value** | **Unit** | **Description** |
|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| $\epsilon$ | 0.1 | N/A | Intermittency control ε. |
